Flight Nanny Information

NANNY FLIGHT

The flight nannies come expertly trained and well equipped. Flight nannies provide pet carriers for safe transport and will deliver any needed documents, as well. Nannies will have food on hand, provided by me, and water – these will be given along the way when needed. The Nanny will communicate with you before, during, and even after the delivery. Clients will be provided with flight numbers and times, updated with pictures, and even videos of your fur baby’s journey home. Nicole French Bulldog will make sure your puppy is well handle by the transportation agency.

THE JOURNEY

While your puppy is traveling from my home to yours , I will make sure to send all necessary details right to your phone. It is my goal to keep everyone on the same page in order to have your puppy have a smooth journey into your arms.

IDENTITY VERIFICATION

For the Clubs protection, I request from all of my travel clients to have ID and credit card verification before the travel arrangements are made. Since I will not be able to verify this information in person, I will request a picture of the cardholder with their Photo ID next to their face, like a selfie, for verification. I will also need a picture of the credit card used for the transaction. I will only need to see the last four of the number as well as the name of the cardholder. Your transaction will need to be paid in full before we book your flight or ship you any extras you may have purchased alongside your fur baby.
