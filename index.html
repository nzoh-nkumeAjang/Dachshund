Welcome To Adorable Dachshund Home

Adorable Dachshund, Strives to create excellent, well tempered pups in every litter. Comfortable in mind for our loving show homes and raising them to be model citizens for our pets homes.

At Adorable Dachshund, we raise AKC Champion Bloodline Dachshund on our twenty acre farm in Vilonia, Arkansas. We are committed to providing healthy, breed-standard, well-tempered companions and furry family members for years to come. We are dedicated to the growth, development, research of the Dachshund breed. To make sure our puppies get the forever home they deserve, we carefully screen potential owners to make sure they understand exactly what is required when it comes to taking care of a Dachshund. At Adorable Welsh Dachshund Home, we continue to care about our animals long after they leave us. As such, it’s our strict policy to only sell to pet homes; we will never sell to pet shops or brokers.
